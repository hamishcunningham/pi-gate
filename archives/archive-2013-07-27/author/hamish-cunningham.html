It's a Jungle Out There

Wed 26 June 2013
By Hamish Cunningham

Tags: pigateraspberrypiraspi

For a couple of years now I've been working on some cheap home computing stuff that might scratch a couple of itches with one stone1:

  • a set-top media center that doesn't cost a fortune, but does have a decent web browser
  • a way of communicating with family and friends that doesn't involve getting all those dodgy adverts from Facebook2
  • a worm-hole generator piped into 1980 and localised on my bald patch

I'm still waiting for the worm-hole thing, but the other stuff is getting very do-able, and the Raspberry Pi is definitely part of the picture. To test out various options I picked up a bunch of different bits of hardware that do jobs similar to the Pi in one way or other... And Fred is busy putting them through their paces for a future article. He's taken some pics:

  1. Mixed metaphors? Love 'em!
  2. "Meet your dream partner at our new site, exclusively for the balding and podgy!" A friend of mine claims that the only reason I get dodgy adverts is because I visit dodgy sites with my cookies turned on, but I'm sure there's no truth in that at all. And I'll sue.
