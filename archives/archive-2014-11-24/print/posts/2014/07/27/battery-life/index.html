AA battery life with the Model A and Model B

   
How long do batteries last with MoPi?.
Tags: kickstarter batteries mo-pi pi-tronics pi raspberrypi gate batteries.
Sun 27 July 2014, Fred Sonnenwald.

We've conducted some tests to help people get a feel for how long AA batteries should power a Rasberry Pi in a variety of configurations when using MoPi. We don't have results from the B+ yet, but that's coming soon...

For now though, the best case scenario? 50 hours using the Model A with 16 AAs!

That's best case, of course, with the Pi not doing anything much, but it does illustrate the potential.
